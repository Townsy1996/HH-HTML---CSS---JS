Halo: Television Series | Season 2 Launch



The highly anticipated second season of the Halo television series has launched on paramount+ today. Although the first season launched with a rather mixed reception, achieving 7.2/10 on IMDB, the showrunners have assured fans that Season 2 will be a grittier, more grounded, more visceral ride. The newly appointed showrunner, David Weiner, also added that What we’re able to do in season two is actually remedy some of the things we would’ve liked to in season one but you don’t really get the chance to during the course of a season. As a bonus, the fans have been treated to not one, but two episodes avaliable for streaming on Paramount + now.

Posted: 8th Febuary 2024



Spirit of Fire Launch | Halo Infinite CU29



Halo Infinite's latest update, content update 29 (CU29) has launched alongside the new Operation: Spirit of Fire. The recent removal of seasons in Halo Infinite has had fans wondering about the future of the title. As part of todays update, all players will receive the classic Mark IV armour Core which has appeared in the likes of Halo Wars, Halo Wars 2 and Halo 5: Guardians.
